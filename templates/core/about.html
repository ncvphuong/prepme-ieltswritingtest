Technology Stack

IELTS Writing Pro is built with modern technology to ensure reliability, security, and performance:

  • Backend: Django Framework with PostgreSQL
  • AI Integration: Claude SDK for advanced language processing
  • Payment Processing: Stripe for secure transactions
  • Frontend: Modern responsive design with Bootstrap

Assessment Criteria

Our AI assessment follows the official IELTS writing criteria:

Task Achievement/Response
How well you address the task requirements and present ideas.
Coherence and Cohesion
How well your ideas are organized and connected.
Lexical Resource
Your vocabulary range and accuracy.
Grammatical Range and Accuracy
Your grammar complexity and correctness.
